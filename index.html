What we offer

About ESports Academy

Founded in 2023, Eleumania ESports Academy is dedicated to helping gamers improve their skills and achieve their goals. We offer a variety of programs, including one-on-one coaching, group classes, and team training sessions.

Vision and Mission

Our Vision

At Eleumania ESports Academy, our vision is to create a world where gamers of all skill levels can come together to learn, grow, and achieve their goals. We believe that gaming has the power to bring people together, and we are committed to building a community where everyone feels welcome and supported.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide the highest quality Esports education and training to gamers around the world. We believe that every gamer has the potential to be great, and we are dedicated to helping our students unlock their full potential. Through personalized coaching, group classes, and team training sessions, we are committed to helping our students achieve their gaming goals and become the best gamers they can be.

We provide

  • One-on-One Coaching

    Our experienced coaches will work with you one-on-one to identify your strengths and weaknesses, and develop a personalized training plan to help you improve your skills.

  • Group Classes

    Our group classes offer a fun and social way to improve your gaming skills. Join a group of gamers at your skill level and learn from our experienced instructors.

  • Team Training Sessions

    Our team training sessions are designed to help your team work together more effectively and achieve your gaming goals. Our experienced coaches will work with your team to identify areas for improvement and develop a customized training plan.
